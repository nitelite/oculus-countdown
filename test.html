Copy the following html tag to insert on your own page:

<iframe src="http://nitelite.github.io/oculus-countdown/index.html" width="500" height="350" border="0" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" style="border: 1px solid red"></iframe>

Adjust width and height so it suits your layout, not all combinations of width and height work equally well though. Remove the style="border: 1px solid red" when you are satified with the width and height.
